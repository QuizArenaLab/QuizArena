
# QuizArena v1.0 Launch Readiness

**Version:** 1.0

**Launch Date:** 15 August 2026

---

# Purpose

Launch Readiness determines whether QuizArena is operationally, technically, commercially, and strategically prepared for public release.

Unlike the Product Roadmap, which defines **what must be built**, Launch Readiness determines **whether the product is genuinely ready for real users.**

A feature being implemented does **not** imply launch readiness.

Launch readiness requires the successful completion of product, engineering, operations, business, marketing, security, and support activities.

---

# Launch Decision Philosophy

Launching a software product is a business decision, not merely an engineering milestone.

QuizArena shall only launch when learners can confidently:

- Discover the platform.
- Understand its value.
- Register successfully.
- Pay securely.
- Participate without friction.
- Receive results immediately.
- Trust the platform.
- Return for future competitions.
