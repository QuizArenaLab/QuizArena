
# QuizArena v1.0 Product Roadmap under ai-product

**Version:** 1.0
**Status:** **LOCKED**
**Product Completion Target:** **26 July 2026**
**Official Public Launch:** **15 August 2026**

---

# Purpose

The Product Roadmap defines every major product area required to successfully launch QuizArena v1.0.

This roadmap serves as the single source of truth for product development and launch execution.

Every engineering sprint, design decision, and business activity must directly support one or more roadmap items.

No new functionality shall be introduced unless it contributes to the successful launch of QuizArena v1.0.

# Product Principles

Every product area must satisfy:

* Functional completeness.
* Excellent user experience.
* Mobile responsiveness.
* Consistent branding.
* Security compliance.
* Production readiness.

No product area shall be considered complete based solely on backend implementation.

---

# Product Quality Standards

Every feature must demonstrate:

* Simplicity
* Speed
* Reliability
* Accessibility
* Scalability
* Maintainability
* Security

---

# Product Success Metrics

QuizArena v1.0 should successfully enable learners to:

* Register.
* Participate.
* Pay securely.
* Compete.
* Receive results instantly.
* View rankings.
* Receive rewards.
* Return for future competitions.

---

# Release Scope (v1.0)

The following product areas are mandatory before launch:

* Identity Platform
* Question Platform
* Competition Platform
* Assessment Experience
* Evaluation Platform
* Commerce Platform
* Rewards Platform
* Administration Platform
* Marketing Platform
* Product Experience
* Security Platform

Analytics may be implemented at a foundational level, with advanced reporting deferred if necessary.

---

# Deferred Beyond v1.0

The following initiatives are planned for future releases:

* AI-generated questions
* Adaptive learning
* Organization management
* Marketplace
* Enterprise subscriptions
* Native mobile applications
* Advanced gamification
* Advanced analytics

---

# Product Completion Definition

QuizArena v1.0 shall be considered complete only when:

* All mandatory product areas are operational.
* The first paid competition can be conducted end-to-end.
* Learners can successfully register, pay, compete, receive results, and claim rewards.
* The platform passes the AI SDLC verification process.
* Security, quality assurance, and production readiness reviews are approved.

---

# Product Governance

This roadmap is governed by:

* QuizArena Product Strategy v1.0
* QuizArena Business Strategy v1.0
* AI SDLC v1.0

Any modification requires formal review and approval.

---

# Roadmap Philosophy (LOCKED)

The purpose of this roadmap is not to maximize the number of implemented features.

The purpose is to deliver a production-ready Competitive Learning Platform that provides exceptional value to learners from the very first competition.

Every roadmap decision shall prioritize:

**Learner Experience → Product Quality → Trust → Retention → Sustainable Growth**
