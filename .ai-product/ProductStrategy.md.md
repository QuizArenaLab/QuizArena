# QuizArena v1.0 Product Roadmap

**Version:** 1.0
**Status:** LOCKED
**Product Completion Target:** 26 July 2026
**Official Launch:** 15 August 2026

---

# Purpose

This roadmap defines every major product area that must be completed before the official public launch of QuizArena v1.0.

It serves as the single execution reference for engineering, product, design, testing, and launch preparation.

The roadmap is derived from the locked Product Strategy and Business Strategy.

No sprint shall introduce functionality outside this roadmap without formal approval.

---

# Product Areas

## 1. Authentication & User Accounts

Objective:

Allow users to securely create accounts, authenticate, manage profiles, and access QuizArena.

Includes:

* Registration
* Login
* Forgot Password
* User Profile
* Session Management
* Authorization

Status:

Completed / Refinement

---

## 2. Question Platform

Objective:

Provide the complete enterprise question management system.

Includes:

* Question Authoring
* Revision Management
* Search
* Import
* Export
* Bulk Operations
* Publishing

Status:

Near Completion

# Execution Principles

Every roadmap item must satisfy the following before completion:

* Functional implementation.
* User experience refinement.
* Mobile responsiveness.
* Integration testing.
* AI SDLC verification.
* Documentation updates.

Completion of code alone does not constitute completion of a roadmap item.

---

# Launch Scope

QuizArena v1.0 will launch only after the following product areas are operational:

* Authentication
* Question Platform
* Competition Management
* Assessment Experience
* Evaluation & Results
* Commerce
* Rewards
* Administration
* Marketing Website
* Product Experience
* Security & Production Readiness

---

# Out of Scope (v1.0)

The following may be deferred to future releases if required:

* AI-powered adaptive learning
* Advanced subscriptions
* Organization management
* Enterprise reporting
* Marketplace
* Mobile applications
* Advanced gamification
