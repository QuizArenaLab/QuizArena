# Artifact Lifecycle

## Governing Authority
All actions are strictly governed by the AI SDLC Constitution:
[.ai-sdlc/constitution/AI-SDLC-v1.1.md](../constitution/AI-SDLC-v1.1.md)

## Progression Order
Artifacts in the AI SDLC are generated in a strict, sequential order. Each artifact depends on the completion of the preceding one.

1. **Implementation Report:** Generated by the Implementation Engineer.
2. **Evidence Report:** Generated by the Evidence Engineer.
3. **Architecture Review:** Generated by the Architecture Reviewer.
4. **QA Review:** Generated by the Quality Assurance Engineer.
5. **Documentation Summary:** Generated by the Documentation Engineer.
6. **Sprint Manifest:** Finalized by the Documentation Engineer.
7. **Sprint Ledger:** Documentation Engineer logs the sprint completion into the SprintLedger.

## Enforcement
The Engineering Manager verifies the presence and correct formatting (e.g., `VERIFIED`, `NOT VERIFIED`, `NOT APPLICABLE`) of each artifact before permitting the sprint to advance to the next stage.
