# Traceability Standard

Defines traceability.

Every requirement shall remain traceable throughout the SDLC.

## Traceability Chain
`Business Idea`
↓
`Capability Specification`
↓
`Business Rules`
↓
`Acceptance Criteria`
↓
`Architecture`
↓
`Implementation`
↓
`Verification`
↓
`Evidence`
↓
`QA`
↓
`Documentation`
↓
`Release`

Nothing shall become detached from its origin.
