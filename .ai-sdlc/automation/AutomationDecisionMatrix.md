# Automation Decision Matrix

Defines when automation proceeds automatically, pauses, or requires specific approval.

## Human Override Matrix
Defines who can override the automation engine.

- **Human Product Owner:** Can override Planning, Cancellation, Priority.
- **Chief Architect:** Can override Architecture, Standards, Workflow.
- **Engineering Manager:** Can override Worker Scheduling, Sprint State, Retries.
- **Implementation Engineer:** Cannot override.
- **Quality Assurance Engineer:** Cannot override.
- **Documentation Engineer:** Cannot override.
